about

中文

Long studied flute from age of 10. Since 2003 Long studied with Prof. Gao Qinping in the High School under Xi'An Conservatory of Music. After gradating from Xi'An in 2006, he got the Full-Scholarship of National University of Singapore Yong Siew Toh Conservatory of Music and Educated Flute with Prof. Jin Ta. During the studying at School, he passed the Freelance audition of Singapore Symphony Orchestra. After bachelor graduating, He was successfully passed the Master Audition of Royal Northern College of Music and Master Orchestra audition of Zurich University of Arts.In 2010, Long started his Master in Zürich University of Arts with Prof. Maria Goldschmidt. In the meantime, he was also passed the Internship audition of Zurich Opera House Orchestra and Invited to play the Music Season Concerts with Zurich Opera House Orchestra. After received the Master Orchestra Degree, Yuan Long has passed the audition of Soloist Diploma Zurich University of Arts . In 2014, Yuan Long got the Soloist diploma of Zurich University with Excellent. ( equal to Performance Doctor).

Long as a Solo Flute joined Guangzhou Orchestra meanwhile as Flute Faculty of Guangzhou Youth Symphony Orchestra. During the time with Guangzhou Symphony Orchestra, he played with a lot of conductors and soloists. such as: Long Yu, Guoyong Zhang, Jia Lv, Daye LIN, Yang Yang, Yi Huang, John Nelson, David Stern, Helene Grimaud, Julien Rachlin, Leonidas Kavakos, Yuja Wang etc. From 2021, Yuan Long has invited to be a Flute Lecturer of United International College. Because of his talented teaching, His students are prize winners of Music Competition. He was also Honor Tutors of BMF Music Competition, The 3rd Korea International Flute Festival Competition,Japan International Music Competition, 2nd Asia International art Competition, The US National Music Competition, etc. Moreover, His student has successfully passed the Audition of Bern University of Arts, TsingHua University China, Manchester University and Indiana University. From Sep, 2023, Long is the Flute Lecturer of The Chinese University of HongKong, Shenzhen School of Music. As the new Faculty of the Chinese University HongKong, Shenzhen School of Music, Long is active between Teaching and Playing. He had been invited as a Flute Soloist to play 2023 International Computer Music Conference and Arnold Schönberg’s 150th Celebration an International Conference. Also He is invited as a Principle flute to play Guangdong-HongKong-Macau Greater Bay Area The 4th International Music Festival. Moreover, as the Faculty member of The Chinese University HongKong, Shenzhen School of Music, He is invited as Soloist to play the Faculty Chamber Music Concert in Shenzhen Concert Hall and The Chinese University HongKong Concert Hall. 2023, Long has been honored Famous Chinese Artist and BiliBili Honored Flute Educator.

Long is also First Prize Winner of Second International Flute Competition Singapore, Excellent Award of IBLA International Music Competition and Third Prize Concerto Competition Singapore. He was a guest flutist of Zurich Opera Orchestra, Singapore Symphony Orchestra and Macau Orchestra, and guest principle flute of Shenzhen Symphony Orchestra and Qingdao Symphony Orchestra.

about

ENG

袁龙自10岁起学习长笛，以优异的成绩考入西安音乐学院附中后跟随长笛教育家高秦平 教授学习。他于2006年获新加坡国立音乐学院杨秀桃音乐学院本科全额奖学金录取，跟随 新加坡交响乐团长笛首席金塔教授学习。在校期间成绩优异，通过了新加坡交响乐团实习 生的考试。2010年，袁龙再以优异的成绩通过了英国北方皇家音乐学院硕士以及瑞士苏黎 世艺术大学的乐队演奏硕士入学考试，师从苏黎世歌剧院首席长笛Maria Goldschmidt- Pahn。在校期间以出色的成绩通过了该校最高独奏家文凭的入学考试，同时还通过了苏黎 世歌剧院乐团的实习考试并多次受邀参与苏黎世歌剧院乐团的乐季音乐会演出。2014年以 最高分的成绩获得该校的最高独奏家文凭(等同于演奏博士学位)。

2014年6月，袁龙通过了广州交响乐团助理首席的招聘考试，任广州交响乐团青年长 笛演奏家，并同时担任广州青年交响乐团的长笛导师。在广州交响乐团9年的工作期间多次 受到国内外著名指挥家，独奏家的赞赏。如：余龍，张国勇，吕嘉，李心草，林大叶，杨 洋，黄屹，John Nelson, David Stern，Helene Grimaud, Julien Rachlin, Leonidas Kavakos, 王羽佳等。自2021年起，袁龙被香港浸会大学与北京师范大学联合国际学院聘任长笛教 师，他的出色教学使他的学生屡次荣获国内外长笛比赛第一名，并且以优异的成绩考入了 瑞士伯尔尼国立音乐学院, 中国清华大学，英国曼彻斯特大学及美国印第安纳大学等。袁龙 还荣获了北京国际音乐节青培艺术全国展演，第三届韩国国际长笛艺术节大赛，日本国际 音乐公开赛，亚洲国际艺术大赛，美国国际音乐比赛，粤港澳大湾区青少年管弦乐大赛优 秀指导教师荣誉。由于其出色的教学成果和卓越的知名度，从2023年9月，袁龙被香港中 文大学(深圳)音乐学院正式聘任为长笛教师，在香港中文大学深圳音乐学院任职期间，袁龙 作为长笛独奏家应邀参加2023年国际计算机音乐大会及全球首个纪念勋伯格150周年纪念 音乐会的演出，并作为学院优秀教师由叶小钢院长亲自带队参加香港中文大学音乐学院的 演出，也收到来自深圳交响乐团的邀请担任特邀长笛首席在世界最高的云颂音乐厅表演。 2023年11月，袁龙被授予为文化榜样中国艺术名家的头衔。2023年12月，袁龙得到了哔哩 哔哩官方认证的“长笛教育家”称号。

在学校教学与乐团演奏外，袁龙亦积极参与国际长笛专业赛事。他在第二届新加坡 国际长笛比赛中获得第一名，在意大利西西里IBLA国际音乐大赛获得优秀奖，并在新加坡 国立大学杨秀桃音乐学院协奏曲比赛获得第三名。他亦曾作为客席长笛演奏家受邀参与苏 黎世歌剧院乐团，新加坡交响乐团及中国澳门乐团，以及特邀长笛首席参加深圳交响乐团 以及青岛交响乐团的演出。
